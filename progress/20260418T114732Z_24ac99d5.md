## Accomplished

Audited `Zstd/Spec/Base.lean` (542 → 519 lines) and `Zstd/Spec/BlockLoop.lean`
for redundancy, brittle `simp` usage, and simplifiable proof steps. Made the
following targeted cleanups:

1. **Removed dead `frame_from_blocks` macro from `Base.lean`** (22 lines). The
   macro was defined but never invoked in `Base.lean`. It remains correctly
   duplicated (`local macro` is file-scoped) in `Content.lean` and
   `TwoBlock.lean`, which do use it.

2. **Simplified `parseFrameHeader_magic` proof** (7 lines → 1 line). Replaced
   manual `cases`-based extraction of `eq_of_beq` with
   `simpa [validMagic] using (Bool.not_eq_true _).mp hmagic`.

3. **Simplified `decompressRawBlock_content` side-condition proof** (6 lines →
   3 lines). Replaced re-unfolding of `decompressRawBlock` in the type-
   ascription proof with reuse of the already-proved helper lemmas
   (`decompressRawBlock_size`, `decompressRawBlock_le_size`,
   `decompressRawBlock_pos_eq`), then closed with `omega`.

4. **Fixed stale module-path references** (leftover `Zip/Spec/...` and
   `ZstdBase.lean` names from before the rename to `Zstd/Spec/...`).

## Decisions

- Kept the 4 structural theorems in `BlockLoop.lean` (`_output_size_ge`,
  `_prefix`, `_pos_gt`, `_le_size`) as-is. They share the same split-at-h
  skeleton, but each inductive leaf differs. Introducing a tactic macro would
  obscure readability and the inline comments documenting each guard are
  useful.
- Kept `unfold_except` duplicated across files. `local macro` is file-scoped,
  and moving it to a shared namespace would be a separate refactor.
- Did not touch `grind`-based proofs (`decompressFrame_contentSize_eq`,
  `decompressFrame_checksum_valid`) — they already have an explanatory
  comment justifying grind over manual case-splitting.
