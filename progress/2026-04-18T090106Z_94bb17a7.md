## Key decisions

- Helper-function approach over `dite` everywhere. Wrapping the bounds
  check inside `readByte` keeps `parseFrameHeader`'s outer structure
  identical to the original (plain `if` guards on size), so the
  existing `iterate 5 split at h` proof script still works. Only the
  terminal step needed the `readByte_eq_getElem` simp lemma.
- Kept `readByte` in `Zstd.Native` (it's implementation), and placed
  the equivalence lemma in `Zstd.Spec.Base` so Frame.lean stays free of
  any `]!` (including doc comments and theorem statements).
